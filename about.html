About PMAY (Gramin)

Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana - Gramin

The Pradhan Mantri Awaas Yojana (Gramin) is a Government of India scheme aimed at providing affordable housing for the rural poor. The objective is to ensure “Housing for All” by 2024 by building pucca houses with basic amenities.

Eligibility

  • Rural families without a pucca house
  • Families listed in SECC 2011 data
  • SC, ST, minorities, and other BPL families

Key Features

  • Financial assistance up to ₹1.20 lakh in plain areas
  • ₹1.30 lakh in hilly/difficult areas
  • Additional assistance for toilets via Swachh Bharat Mission

Benefits

  • Safe and secure housing
  • Improved quality of life
  • Empowerment of women (house in their name)
